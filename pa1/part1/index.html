Horse

The horse is a domesticated, hoofed mammal. Humans have been domesticating horses ~4000 BCE, likely beginning in central Asia. Due to their place in the lives of humans, they have been transported all throughout the modern world.

Lifespan and stages of life

The modern domestic horse has a lifespan between 25 and 30 years, occasionally living into their 40s or beyond.
